The following communication was first made available to Udemy personnel on March 10, 2026:

Udemates,

Earlier today, we announced that both Udemy and Coursera will hold special shareholder meetings on April 9 to vote on our planned combination. For the Udemy special meeting, we require approval from a majority of our shareholders in order to complete the transaction.

Next Steps

If you were a vested Udemy stockholder as of March 6, 2026 (the record date for our special meeting), you’ll receive instructions from your bank or broker about how to vote your Udemy shares at our special meeting.

You can continue to expect regular updates over the next few weeks to keep you informed. Nothing changes about your role, your team, or your day-to-day work right now as a result of today’s announcement. This is simply the next step in the process.

Thank you for your continued focus and commitment as we work toward this exciting next chapter for Udemy.

Hugo
